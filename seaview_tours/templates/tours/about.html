Who We Are

SeaView Tours & Travel is a Uganda-based travel company dedicated to providing exceptional travel solutions for both local and international travelers. Founded with a passion for exploration and customer satisfaction, we have grown to become one of Uganda's most trusted travel agencies.

We specialize in making travel accessible, safe, and memorable for everyone. Whether you're planning a wildlife safari, need visa processing assistance, or looking for the best flight deals, our team of experienced professionals is here to guide you every step of the way.

Our commitment to excellence and customer-centric approach has earned us the trust of thousands of travelers who have experienced Uganda's beauty through our carefully curated tours and services.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To make travel across Uganda easy, safe, and memorable for all tourists through exceptional service, reliable travel solutions, and personalized attention to every client's needs.

Our Vision

To become the leading travel company in East Africa, recognized for quality, reliability, and unforgettable travel experiences that showcase the beauty of Uganda to the world.

Our Story

SeaView Tours & Travel is a premier travel agency based in Uganda, founded by industry expert Miiro.

With years of experience in travel and tourism, we've grown from a small local agency to a comprehensive travel solutions provider serving clients across Uganda and internationally.

Our team combines local expertise with global travel knowledge to deliver exceptional service for every journey.
